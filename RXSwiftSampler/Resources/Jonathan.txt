Jonathan awoke to a typical Saturday morning from an unsettled night.  The 14 year old had watched the Jules Verne’s classic 20,000 Leagues Under the Sea which contrasts where he is living, in the confines of a large cylinder.   Jonathan’s world consists of a few small towns amongst pasturelands laid out within a huge cylinder nearly as far as he could see.   He can barely make out the neighboring communities high above, thru the clouds on the opposite side of the cylinder.
